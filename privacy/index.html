StepArc

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

StepArc is an iOS fitness app for walking-based training, foot health tracking, load management, and virtual mountain progress. This Privacy Policy explains what information StepArc collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

StepArc is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

Information We Collect

Information you enter in the app

StepArc may store information you provide, including:

  • First name or display name
  • Onboarding segment, such as foot pain, knee pain, running injury, standing all day, or general fitness
  • Balance hold time, heel raise count, walking endurance, pain level, and optional navicular drop measurement
  • Training sessions, readiness checks, pain journal entries, footwear logs, achievements, and mountain progress
  • Notification preferences

This information is used to personalize StepArc, calculate your Arc Score, track progress, adapt training guidance, and show trends over time.

HealthKit information

With your permission, StepArc reads limited Apple Health data:

  • Step count
  • Flights climbed and elevation-related activity data where available

StepArc uses HealthKit data only for app functionality, including walking load, weekly progression, elevation credit, and virtual mountain progress. StepArc does not sell HealthKit data, use HealthKit data for advertising, or share HealthKit data with affiliates or other third parties for marketing.

You can grant, deny, or revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in the Apple Health app or iOS Settings.

Purchases

StepArc does not currently offer in-app purchases in this beta. If purchases, subscriptions, or trials are added later, this policy will be updated before those features are made available.

Device and diagnostics information

StepArc may use Apple-provided system logs and crash diagnostics available through Apple developer tools to diagnose app reliability issues. These diagnostics may include device model, operating system version, app version, and crash details.

How We Use Information

StepArc uses information to:

  • Create and personalize your profile
  • Calculate and display Arc Score and related trends
  • Track walking load, training progress, pain trends, and mountain progress
  • Provide reminders and notifications you choose to enable
  • Improve app reliability and debug technical issues
  • Comply with legal, safety, and App Store obligations

Where Information Is Stored

Most StepArc training and health-related information is stored locally on your device using Apple's local storage frameworks. At this stage, StepArc does not provide user accounts or cloud sync.

If StepArc adds account sync, AI coaching, cloud backup, purchases, or subscriptions in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features transmit additional personal or health-related information off your device.

Sharing

StepArc does not sell your personal information.

StepArc may share limited information with:

  • Apple, for HealthKit permission controls, App Store platform services, TestFlight, and diagnostics
  • Service providers who help operate, secure, or debug the app, only as needed for those services
  • Legal or safety authorities if required by law or necessary to protect users, StepArc, or others

StepArc may include links or recommendations for footwear, orthotics, or related products. If affiliate links are added or used, clicking those links may take you to a third-party site with its own privacy practices. StepArc does not share HealthKit data with affiliate partners.

Health and Safety

StepArc is designed for general fitness and education. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, physical therapy, or emergency care. If you have severe pain, worsening symptoms, swelling, numbness, inability to bear weight, or other concerning symptoms, seek professional medical advice.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Stop using StepArc at any time
  • Delete app data by deleting the app from your device
  • Revoke HealthKit access in the Apple Health app or iOS Settings
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings
  • Contact us to ask privacy questions

Because StepArc currently stores most app data locally on your device, deleting the app may permanently delete local StepArc data unless Apple or iOS has retained a device backup.

Children's Privacy

StepArc is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

If you use StepArc outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where Apple or other service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as StepArc changes. If changes are material, we will update the effective date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the app or through the App Store.
