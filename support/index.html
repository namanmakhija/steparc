Health and Safety

StepArc is for general fitness and education. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, physical therapy, or emergency services.

If you have severe pain, worsening symptoms, swelling, numbness, inability to bear weight, chest pain, shortness of breath, or any urgent medical concern, stop using the app and seek professional medical care.

Helpful Details to Include

  • What you were trying to do
  • What you expected to happen
  • What happened instead
  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • Whether Health permissions are enabled for StepArc
